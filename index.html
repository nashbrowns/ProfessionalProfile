    • About Me

    Hi, I'm Nash McDonald. I was born and raised in Bend, Oregon where I learned to love skiing, rock climbing, and skateboarding. When I'm not doing these, you can find me hanging out with friends, playing videogames, making beer, and working on my own personal projects.

    I moved to Portland, Oregon where I got my B.S. in Electrical Engineering at Portland State University. I recently started a fullstack web development bootcamp through the University of Oregon to hone my programming skills and to learn about back-end web systems and databases. I'd like to use this knowledge to pursue a career in IOT.

    Portland is still the place I call home, and I have fallen in love with this city since I moved here in 2012. There is so much to explore here, and so many creative people and activities to get involved with. I look forward to continuing my professional career in this beautiful city!
